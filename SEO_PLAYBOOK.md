# ZAYBAASH SEO Playbook

## Primary Goal
- Make branded query performance dominant for "ZAYBAASH" and improve non-branded visibility for commercial category terms.

## Target Keywords (Priority Order)
- zaybaash
- zaybaash official store
- women dresses pakistan
- premium women fashion pakistan
- one piece dresses pakistan
- two piece suits pakistan
- luxury women clothing pakistan
- signature dress pakistan

## URL Targeting Map
- `/` for brand terms and broad fashion intent.
- `/dresses` for dresses and category-commercial intent.
- `/shop` for broad shopping and discovery intent.
- `/signature-dress` for high-intent hero product discovery.
- `/product/[id]` for long-tail product intent.

## Internal Linking Rules
- Homepage should link to `/dresses`, `/shop`, and `/signature-dress` in above-the-fold sections.
- Every product page should link to at least one category page and one related product.
- Footer links should include `/about`, `/contact`, `/faq`, and `/size-guide`.
- Avoid linking to noindex pages from key SEO landing pages unless needed for user flow.

## Content Publishing Plan (Next 8 Weeks)
- Week 1: Category copy refresh for `/dresses` with 400-700 words of unique, buyer-focused content.
- Week 2: Improve `/signature-dress` with craftsmanship, fit, and delivery copy.
- Week 3: Publish style guide content targeting seasonal and occasion queries.
- Week 4: Publish fit/size content and interlink to `/size-guide`.
- Week 5: Publish delivery and payment trust content and interlink to `/faq`.
- Week 6: Add collection highlights and intent-based internal links from homepage blocks.
- Week 7: Refresh top product descriptions with unique value and care details.
- Week 8: Re-audit internal links and update underperforming pages.

## Search Console Workflow
- Submit sitemap: `https://www.zaybaash.com/sitemap.xml`.
- Request indexing for `/`, `/dresses`, `/shop`, `/signature-dress`, and top 20 product URLs.
- Monitor Coverage and Core Web Vitals weekly.
- Check Performance report for branded vs non-branded query trends every 7 days.

## KPI Targets (First 90 Days)
- Branded clicks up by 30%+.
- Homepage average position for branded query at 1-2.
- Indexed product URLs above 90% of active catalog.
- CTR improvement on top landing pages by 10%+.

## Do Not Do
- Do not keyword-stuff titles or descriptions.
- Do not index login/account/checkout/cart/private pages.
- Do not publish duplicate product descriptions across multiple SKUs.
